Step 1 — Configure Settings

Do once

Open the Settings tab and configure these before your first trip. Everything saves automatically.

1

Fuel Type

Pick Petrol, Diesel, CNG, or Electric. This changes the vehicle presets, fuel price field, and unit labels throughout the app.

2

Platform Commission

Tap Bolt (20%), Uber (25%), or InDrive (7.5%) to load the preset rate, or drag the slider for a custom percentage.

3

Vehicle & Efficiency

Choose your car from the Vehicle dropdown for a known km/L value, or select your Engine Size to auto-set a typical efficiency. Fine-tune with the +/− stepper.

4

Air Conditioning

Toggle AC on if you usually drive with the air con running. Set the efficiency penalty % — typically 15–20% — and the app reduces your km/L automatically.

5

Maintenance & Wear

Set a ₦ per km rate to account for tyres, oil, and servicing. ₦20–₦50/km is a common range. The cost is multiplied by each trip's distance.

6

Fixed Daily Costs

Enter recurring daily expenses — car loan, data subscription, parking/toll, car wash. These are subtracted from your gross profit at the end of the day to show your real take-home.

Step 2 — Log a Trip

Per trip
1

Enter Gross Earnings

On the Log tab, type the amount the platform paid you — the number shown in your Bolt, Uber, or InDrive app before any deductions.

2

Add Distance & Duration

Enter the trip distance (km) and time driven (hours). The preview box instantly calculates your platform fee, fuel cost, maintenance, and net profit in real time.

3

Read the Preview

Check Rev/km, Cost/km, Profit/km, and Margin before adding. Green margin = healthy trip. Red margin = this trip is costing you money.

4

Add Trip to Ledger

Tap Add Trip to Ledger. The header animates with your updated daily profit, total distance, hours, and hourly wage. Fields clear automatically for the next trip.

Step 3 — Ledger & Statement

1

Review Your Trips

The Today tab lists every trip logged today. Each row shows gross, platform fee, fuel used, and net profit. The economics strip shows Rev/km, Cost/km, Profit/km, and Margin for that trip.

2

Delete or Clear

Tap the trash icon on any trip to remove it. Tap Clear Day to reset the entire ledger and start fresh.

3

View Today's Statement

Tap View Today's Statement at the top of Today. This sliding panel shows your full-day breakdown: Gross → Platform Fees → Fuel → Maintenance → Fixed Costs → Take-Home Profit and hourly wage.

4

Break-Even Banner

The coloured banner below your daily total tells you how far you still need to earn to cover all costs — or how much above break-even you are.

5

Share to WhatsApp

Inside Today's Statement, tap Share Summary on WhatsApp. On mobile it shares a screenshot of your statement. On desktop it sends a formatted text report with all figures.

Install as Web App

No store needed

Add DriverProfit to your home screen for a full-screen app experience that works offline.

Android — Chrome
  • Tap the ⋮ menu (top-right of Chrome)
  • Select "Add to Home Screen"
  • Or tap the "Install App" banner if it appears at the bottom
  • Tap Add to confirm
iPhone / iPad — Safari
  • Tap the Share button ◻↑ at the bottom of Safari
  • Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  • Tap Add to confirm
Desktop — Chrome / Edge
  • Look for the install icon ⊕ in the browser address bar
  • Click it and select "Install"
Works offline after first install. All trip data is stored locally on your device until you choose to back it up.
